Already Dead

From the EP "The Righteous Heart of Winter"

The foul magic of the wizard has consumed the lowlands.
Their plains, farms, towns and homes lie ruined,
smoldering in the endless night.
All hope has left the hearts of the people,
no heroes are left to hear their wails of despair and suffering.
But, unbeknownst to the piteous masses, there is one who may yet save them. Less than a man but more than a mere ghost,
the lonely warrior who walks beyond death seeks allies in his quest for vengeance.
A vision given by the old gods grips his mind.
A vast waste of icy desert, a wall of impenetrable peaks,
an ancient stronghold and a black shadow lying in wait.
Death walks, slowly, unfaltering, to the frozen north.

Through the swamp

From the EP "The Swamplord's Curse"

Power.
Power is the curse that ruins the hearts of the righteous.
And now it is power that the Deathsurvivor seeks, power to rival the dread magics of the Void Breathers and their evil servant.
To the eastern swampland they ride. There is no dread ruler or cruel king here, there are only whispers.
A seeping corruption and a legend of a hermit, who probed the strange eddies of reality that permeate the twisted marsh and found in them a force that no living soul should possess.
A deal will be made, but at what price?
The consequences will echo through space and time...
