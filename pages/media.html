Already dead
The foul magic of the wizard has consumed the lowlands. Their plains, farms, towns and homes lie ruined, smoldering in endless night. All hope has left the hearts of the people, no heroes are left to hear their wails of despair and suffering. But, unbeknownst to the piteous masses, there is one who may yet save them. Less than a man but more than a mere ghost, the lonely warrior who walks beyond death seeks allies in his quest for vengeance. A vision given by the old gods grips his mind. A vast waste of icy desert, a wall of impenetrable peaks, an ancient stronghold and a black shadow lying in wait. Death walks, slowly, unfaltering, to the frozen north.
Through the swamp
Power. Power is the curse that ruins the hearts of the righteous. Power is the corrupter, the addiction that sees Great Rulers twist and debase themselves until they are little more than tyrants. And now it is power that the Deathsurvivor seeks, power to rival the dread magics of the Void Breathers and their evil servant. To the east they ride, into a rotting, stagnant place, devoid of civilization. Only beasts and beings of unnatural strangeness survive in The Swamp. There is no dread ruler or cruel king here, there are only whispers. A seeping corruption and a half remembered legend of a hermit, who probed the strange eddies of reality that permeate the twisted swamplands and found in them a force that no living soul should possess. A deal will be made, but at what price? There is no other choice. The consequences will echo through space and time...
The last ride of Deathsurvivor
The Deathsurvivor rides. Flanked by his unliving allies and wielding the dread power of the Swamp Lich, he goes to his death and destiny. Mercy is alien to those serving under the shadow of the sorcerer's tower, and gladly they shall have no need of it in dispatching the Dark Wizard. In the Valley of the Wizard dwells evil unbound. The time has come to bind it. With the quest fulfilled, the reaper will come for him at last... surely...
